Chilli Fish

Description

Chilli Fish is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that combines crispy fried fish with a spicy, tangy sauce. This dish is known for its bold flavors, featuring a mix of soy sauce, chili sauce, and a hint of sweetness. It’s perfect as an appetizer or a main course, served with steamed rice or noodles. The vibrant colors and tantalizing aroma make it a favorite at any gathering.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams boneless fish fillets, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon pepper powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons curd (yogurt)
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil (for sauce)
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 capsicum (bell pepper), chopped
  • 4 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons green chili sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder

Steps

  1. Marinate the Fish: In a bowl, combine the fish pieces with lemon juice, turmeric powder, chili powder, pepper powder, ginger-garlic paste, and curd. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Batter: In another bowl, mix the all-purpose flour, cornflour, and a pinch of salt. Add a little water to make a thick batter.
  3. Fry the Fish: Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Dip the marinated fish pieces in the batter and fry until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. Make the Sauce: In a separate pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add the chopped onion, capsicum, and green chilies. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  5. Add Sauces and Spices: Add the green chili sauce, tomato ketchup, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and black pepper powder to the pan. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes.
  6. Combine Fish and Sauce: Add the fried fish pieces to the pan and toss to coat them evenly with the sauce. Add ½ cup of water and let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the fish well.
  7. Serve: Transfer the chilli fish to a serving dish and garnish with chopped spring onions or cilantro if desired. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.
